# Ways to Donate

## Online

Click [here](https://giving.servantkeeper.com/chfm/) to go to our online
donation site and:

1. Select **ONE-TIME** or **RECURRING** contribution; specify an amount.
2. Click Continue.
3. Log in or create a new account.
4. Select Payment Method: [Bank Account]({{site.baseurl}}/donate/bank-account.html) or Credit Card

Note: The fee for bank account donations is lower than the fee for credit card donations (1% vs. 2.89%).

Note: The online donation site will not connect if you are using a VPN.
